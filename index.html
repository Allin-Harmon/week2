One of my favorite websites is PCPartPicker.com.

Check it out. Click this link or the button

It is a great site if you are planning to build a computer because:

  1. You can check compatibility with various parts
    • The site can tell you if the ram you want to use is compatible with your motherboard.
    • It can tell you if your motherboard is compatible with your processor.
    • It can tell you if your case is large enough for your CPU cooler
  2. You can see other builds and get ideas for your own build
  3. You can get an idea of the price of your build
  4. You can mock up a build and order all of the parts at once

I also like Linus Tech Tips to help me stay up on some of the latest trends in technology and the PC building world.
